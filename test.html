What Solo Leveling is?

Solo Leveling is easily the most popular manhwa series out there with many intriguing mysteries that are only explained towards its ending. The widespread success of the anime adaptation alone speaks volumes of Solo Leveling's simple yet immersive story. That said, despite its popularity and loyal fanbase, the series' ending takes an unexpected path that left many fans shocked and even unsatisfied.
